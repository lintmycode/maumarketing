# MAU Marketing service page specs v1

Status: draft
Date: 2026-04-07

## Purpose

Define the first 3 service pages for MAU Marketing in a way that is commercially clear, easy to build, and easy to sell.

These pages should:
- speak to visible business pain
- avoid abstract agency language
- make scope feel tangible
- support both direct inquiry and later productized pricing

---

# Service 1 — Landing pages e websites de conversão

## Core promise
Create pages that help a business present an offer clearly and turn more visits into contacts, bookings, or leads.

## Best-fit client
- businesses launching or promoting a specific service
- businesses with weak websites
- businesses driving traffic to pages that do not convert well
- businesses needing a campaign page quickly

## Problems this page should speak to
- the website looks fine but does not help sell
- traffic lands on generic pages with weak CTAs
- service pages are confusing or bloated
- there is no clear next step for the visitor
- forms/contact flow are poor

## Deliverables to mention
Depending on scope:
- landing page
- sales/service page
- contact/lead capture flow
- thank-you page
- form + CRM connection
- performance and UX fixes
- copy structure support

## Message direction
This is not “just web design.”
This is web work focused on:
- clarity
- conversion
- contact generation
- less friction

## Suggested structure
1. Hero
2. Common problems
3. What is included
4. How the work happens
5. Who this is for
6. CTA

## Offer variants
- one-off landing page
- landing page sprint
- website-as-sales-tool improvement sprint

## Upsell paths
- Local Visibility Fix
- CRM Follow-up Fix
- monthly campaign support

# Service 2 — Visibilidade local e melhorias práticas

## Core promise
Improve how a local business appears online and fix the obvious issues that reduce trust, visibility, and contact.

## Best-fit client
- local businesses
- service businesses
- businesses depending on local discovery and trust
- businesses with outdated Google Business Profile or weak local presence

## Problems this page should speak to
- poor or inconsistent Google Business presence
- weak local search visibility
- slow site/performance problems
- outdated business information
- poor location/service page structure
- bad first impression on mobile

## Deliverables to mention
Depending on scope:
- Google Business Profile review and optimization
- local SEO quick audit
- GTmetrix/performance audit
- trust/friction review
- location/service page recommendations
- implementation of agreed fixes

## Message direction
This is a practical service for businesses whose online presence is making them look worse than they are.

Avoid promising SEO miracles.
Promise:
- clarity
- hygiene
- trust improvements
- practical visibility fixes

## Suggested structure
1. Hero
2. Symptoms and common issues
3. What gets reviewed and fixed
4. Why this matters commercially
5. Who this is for
6. CTA

## Offer variants
- visibility audit only
- audit + fixes sprint
- ongoing local visibility maintenance

## Upsell paths
- landing page work
- CRM follow-up improvements
- monthly maintenance retainer

# Service 3 — CRM e automação de follow-up

## Core promise
Help businesses stop losing leads after first contact by improving CRM structure, follow-up, and operational flow.

## Best-fit client
- businesses getting inquiries by form, email, WhatsApp, or phone
- businesses with manual or messy lead handling
- businesses without a clear pipeline
- businesses wanting EspoCRM or needing it configured properly

## Problems this page should speak to
- leads are handled manually
- nobody knows what stage each lead is in
- follow-up depends on memory
- WhatsApp/email are chaotic
- no system exists for reminders, statuses, or next steps
- current CRM is weak, unused, or badly configured

## Deliverables to mention
Depending on scope:
- lead-flow review
- pipeline design
- EspoCRM setup
- EspoCRM customization
- simple automations
- reminders / next-step logic
- hosted or managed CRM option
- selective AI workflow integration where useful

## Message direction
This service is about operational sanity and lost-opportunity reduction.
Not “digital transformation.”
Not corporate theatre.
Just a better system for handling real leads.

## Suggested structure
1. Hero
2. Typical chaos patterns
3. What gets improved
4. What EspoCRM / workflow setup can include
5. Who this is for
6. CTA

## Offer variants
- CRM audit
- setup/customization sprint
- setup + monthly management

## Upsell paths
- landing page improvements
- AI workflow add-ons
- reporting/optimization retainer

# Shared page rules

## Tone
- direct
- practical
- anti-fluff
- confident
- accessible to normal business owners

## Avoid
- exaggerated promises
- vague “360” language
- too much technical jargon up front
- long copy blocks with no structure

## Reusable sections across all service pages
- problem framing
- what is included
- who it is for
- process
- CTA

## CTA style
Good CTA language:
- Pedir proposta
- Falar sobre isto
- Ver se faz sentido
- Explicar o problema

Avoid CTA language that sounds too salesy or fake-urgent.

## Future expansion ideas
Later service pages could include:
- AI integrations
- audits and quick wins
- campaign launch support
- content systems
- reporting and measurement
