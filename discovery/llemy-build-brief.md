# MAU Marketing — build brief for Llemy

Build the first public website for **MAU Marketing**, a **Nitida Digital brand**.

## Context

MAU Marketing is a marketing-facing branch of Nitida. It should feel distinct, sharper, more playful, and more explicitly commercial than Nitida, but still grounded in real delivery capability.

The offer is **not** “full-service marketing agency for everything.”
It is a tighter, practical service business for small/local businesses that need:
- better landing pages / conversion websites
- better local visibility / Google Business / practical SEO fixes
- better CRM and follow-up systems
- selective AI-enabled operational improvements

The tone should be:
- direct
- slightly irreverent
- anti-BS
- credible
- not childish
- not too insider-ish

## Audience

Primary audience:
- Portuguese small businesses
- local businesses
- service businesses
- businesses frustrated with weak current marketing
- businesses that can pay for more than a cheap brochure website

Secondary audience:
- more sophisticated brands / businesses who get the irony of the name and appreciate a sharper tone

## Language

Important:
- site should support **Portuguese and English**
- **Portuguese is primary**
- English should exist, but PT should lead the voice, homepage thinking, and information architecture
- do not make the English version just a rigid literal translation if a line depends on Portuguese nuance

## Domain / brand notes

- domain likely: `maumarketing.pt`
- brand should be presented as **MAU Marketing**
- do not over-explain the acronym or force a fake expansion
- let the name create curiosity, but resolve it fast with clear commercial copy
- include the line **“A Nitida Digital brand”** in appropriate places

## Positioning

The site must communicate quickly that:
- this is not generic agency fluff
- this is not AI slop
- this is practical marketing execution
- websites are treated as marketing tools, not decorative artifacts
- AI helps internally, but humans remain responsible for judgment and quality

## Core services for launch

Focus the launch site around these 3 offers:

### 1) Landing pages and conversion websites
Problem solved:
- weak websites
- weak service pages
- traffic landing on pages that do not convert
- unclear offers and weak CTAs

### 2) Local visibility and practical improvements
Problem solved:
- weak Google Business profile
- poor local presence
- avoidable SEO/performance/friction issues
- poor first impression for local businesses

### 3) CRM and follow-up systems
Problem solved:
- messy lead handling
- no clear pipeline
- weak follow-up
- overreliance on memory / WhatsApp / email chaos
- EspoCRM setup/customization opportunity

Optional adjacent capability to mention lightly:
- practical AI integrations when there is real operational value

## Build goals

The homepage should achieve these fast:
1. explain what MAU Marketing is
2. make the provocative name feel intentional
3. present concrete services
4. make contact easy

The whole website should feel:
- lean
- fast
- structured
- commercially useful
- easy to extend later

## Technical direction

Use Nitida-style stack assumptions as a reference.

Preferred direction:
- WordPress
- Astra as the base theme
- lightweight implementation
- avoid plugin bloat
- avoid overbuilt page-builder complexity
- use reusable patterns/components
- strong mobile UX
- performance-conscious build

Assume this is a clean business site, not a bloated funnel machine.

## Initial sitemap

Launch with:
- Home
- Services
- 3 service pages
- How it works
- About
- Contact

Optional/later:
- case studies
- articles/insights

## Homepage requirements

The homepage should likely include:
- hero
- clear value proposition
- 3 core service blocks
- “web as a marketing tool” explanation
- “AI, but supervised” explanation
- why MAU is different from generic agencies
- credibility / Nitida relationship
- CTA

Hero headline direction can be explored, but the page must not spend too long explaining the name.

Possible PT angle examples:
- “Provavelmente não precisas de mais marketing. Precisas de marketing melhor.”
- “Websites, landing pages e sistemas de follow-up para negócios que já estão fartos de conversa.”
- “MAU Marketing. Porque muito do que por aí anda é mesmo mau marketing.”

These are directional, not mandatory final copy.

## Service page expectations

Create first-class pages for:

### A) Landing pages e websites de conversão
Should communicate:
- this is not just web design
- this is web work aimed at clarity, conversion, contact generation, and less friction

### B) Visibilidade local e melhorias práticas
Should communicate:
- this is not fake SEO bravado
- this is a practical service to improve trust, visibility, and local online hygiene

### C) CRM e automação de follow-up
Should communicate:
- this is operational sanity
- this is about stopping lead leakage
- EspoCRM is a credible implementation path

## Copy guidance

Avoid:
- empty agency language
- exaggerated AI claims
- “360 solutions” nonsense
- too much jargon
- long blocks of text with no structure

Prefer:
- concrete pain points
- practical outcomes
- direct language
- fast clarity
- slight bite, but still accessible to normal business owners

## AI positioning

Be explicit but sane:
- AI helps with speed, research, drafting, structure, and internal acceleration
- humans remain responsible for review, judgment, and delivery
- do not position MAU as an autonomous AI agency

## Relationship with Nitida

This should be visible and positive.
Use wording like:
- **A Nitida Digital brand**

But MAU should still feel like:
- its own brand
- its own tone
- its own workflow
- its own site/domain

## Deliverables requested from Llemy

Produce a practical implementation-ready package, not just vague ideas.

Please create:
1. proposed site structure / information architecture
2. homepage copy draft in PT + EN
3. page-by-page content outlines
4. first-pass service page copy drafts in PT + EN
5. brand/visual direction recommendations
6. technical build spec for WordPress + Astra
7. component/section system for reuse
8. implementation plan

## Constraints

- keep it lean
- do not over-engineer
- do not make it look like generic AI startup sludge
- do not overcomplicate the CMS
- optimize for fast launch and easy future iteration
