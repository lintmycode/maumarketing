# MAU Marketing homepage wireframe v1

Status: draft
Date: 2026-04-07

## Goal

Create a homepage that makes **MAU Marketing** feel intentional, credible, sharp, and commercially useful within a few seconds.

The homepage must do four things quickly:
1. explain what MAU Marketing is
2. make the provocative name feel deliberate rather than amateur
3. show concrete services
4. get the visitor to contact MAU

## Core positioning

MAU Marketing helps small businesses improve how they present themselves, capture leads, and follow up — using practical websites, landing pages, local visibility improvements, CRM systems, and useful automation.

Tone:
- direct
- playful / irreverent
- not childish
- anti-BS
- commercially clear

## Section 2 — What MAU actually does

Translate the brand into concrete commercial understanding.

### Intro line
**Menos marketing de palco. Mais páginas úteis, visibilidade local e follow-up que não deixa contactos morrer.**

### 3-column service summary
#### Card 1 — Landing pages e websites de conversão
For businesses that need a page to sell, capture, or launch.

#### Card 2 — Visibilidade local e melhorias práticas

#### Card 3 — CRM e automação de follow-up

Each card should include:
- one-line problem statement
- one-line result statement
- link to service page

## Section 3 — Is this for you?

### Goal
Qualify visitors quickly.

### Format
Checklist-style or short bullets.

Example copy direction:
MAU Marketing pode ser uma boa ideia se:
- tens um site fraco ou que não te ajuda a vender

## Section 4 — Web as a marketing tool

### Goal
Reframe web development as part of the marketing system.

### Message
A website should not be sold as a decorative object.
It should help:
- explain the offer
- capture attention
- route leads
- reduce friction
- support follow-up

This section reinforces the bridge between NÍTIDA’s existing strengths and MAU’s marketing positioning.

## Notes on tone

The homepage should not over-explain the name.
It should acknowledge the tension and move quickly into commercial clarity.

Do:
- write with confidence
- be specific
- let the name create curiosity
- resolve the curiosity with useful language quickly

Do not:
- spend half the homepage explaining the joke
- become too insider-ish
- become too ironic to trust
- bury the services under branding cleverness

## Conversion goal

Primary conversion:
- contact form inquiry

Secondary conversion:
- email click
- WhatsApp click if used
